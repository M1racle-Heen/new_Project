ABOUT

Photography John Doe

My name is John Doe and I am a 22 year old photographer from Almaty, Kazakhstan. I grew up in Shymkent and moved to Almaty in 2017. It was after moving to Almaty that I discovered my passion for photography, when I discovered the beautiful Almaty nature. I gave my first photography workshop in 2019 and now leading others has become a big part of my journey towards photography. I decided that I wanted to learn more about photography, and in 2020 I started studying visual marketing in Almaty. Today I still shoot landscapes, but I also work for different companies for marketing and advertising purposes. My work has been published in more than 15 different countries around the world. Feel free to contact if you are interested in something. I speak English, Russian, Kazakh and I know Turkish well.
